# Litbuy Spreadsheet Resources

Welcome to my GitHub profile.

This account focuses on building and maintaining organized shopping resources, spreadsheet-style directories, and product discovery guides for Litbuy users.

## Featured Resource

### Litbuy Spreadsheet 2026

The Litbuy Spreadsheet is designed to make product discovery more organized by bringing curated finds and shopping categories into one structured directory.

Current areas of focus include:

* sneakers and footwear
* hoodies and jackets
* T-shirts and everyday clothing
* pants and shorts
* bags and accessories
* seasonal fashion finds
* organized shopping references

## Why This Project Exists

Finding products manually can require searching through scattered posts, seller pages, bookmarks, and older spreadsheets.

The goal of this project is to create a cleaner browsing experience where users can explore categorized products without opening dozens of unrelated pages.
